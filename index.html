Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to NutriWorkout Companion. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and store the following types of data locally on your device:

  • Camera and Media: If you grant permission, we may access your device’s camera and media storage to enable certain features, such as capturing and saving photos or videos within the app.
  • Notifications: We may use your device’s notification system to send you alerts and reminders based on your preferences.
  • Network State: We may access your device’s network state to determine the availability of internet connectivity for specific features.
  • Alarm Scheduling: We may access your device’s alarm scheduling capabilities to enable scheduled notifications and reminders.

3. How We Use Your Information

All data collected is currently stored locally on your device and is used solely for the purpose of providing and enhancing the functionality of the app, including:

  • Enabling the use of the camera and media features.
  • Sending you notifications and reminders.
  • Scheduling alarms for specific events or reminders.

4. Data Storage and Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not transmit, store, or process any personal data on our servers. We implement security measures to protect your information on your device.

5. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Internet Access: To check network connectivity.
  • Post Notifications: To send notifications.
  • Schedule Exact Alarm: To schedule alarms.
  • Vibrate: To enable vibration for notifications.
  • Read External Storage: To access media files stored on your device.
  • Camera: To capture photos and videos.
  • Access Network State: To check the network status.
  • Receive Boot Completed: To reset alarms and notifications after the device restarts.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
